About Cirno

Cirno is an ice fairy living in the land of Gensokyo. Despite her childish appearance, she possesses powerful ice-based abilities, making her a force to be reckoned with.

Cirno's Antics

Cirno is known for her mischievous and playful personality. She often gets into trouble but always manages to find her way out with her quick thinking and ice powers.

Cirno's Relationships

Cirno has various relationships with other characters in Gensokyo. She is often seen interacting with characters like Reimu Hakurei, Marisa Kirisame, and Letty Whiterock.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Cirno?
Cirno is an ice fairy and one of the most well-known characters from the Touhou Project series, created by Team Shanghai Alice. She is often depicted as childish and somewhat naive but possesses incredible ice-based abilities.
What are Cirno's abilities?
Cirno has the ability to manipulate ice and cold temperatures. She can create and control ice in various forms, such as ice bullets, barriers, and freezing spells. Despite her childish nature, her powers are formidable.
Is Cirno a protagonist or antagonist?
Cirno's role varies depending on the game or story within the Touhou Project series. While she is often portrayed as mischievous and playful, she can also be seen as a protagonist in some scenarios, helping others or going on her own adventures.
